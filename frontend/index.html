Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
What is SupportIQ and how does it help NDIS providers?+
SupportIQ is an AI-powered platform built exclusively for Australian NDIS service providers. It automates NDIS billing and PRODA claims, SCHADS Award payroll, DEX/DSS reporting, rostering, and audit readiness using 30+ purpose-built AI agents — eliminating manual processes and compliance errors.
How does SupportIQ handle NDIS billing and PRODA claim submissions?+
SupportIQ's AI billing agents automatically validate every claim against the current NDIS Price Guide before submission to PRODA. This eliminates billing errors, wrong support item codes, mismatched rates, and expired plan dates — reducing rejection rates to near-zero.
Is SupportIQ compliant with the SCHADS Award 2025?+
Yes. SupportIQ includes a full SCHADS Award interpretation engine covering all employee classifications, penalty rates, allowances, overtime rules, and SWS wage assessments. The system is automatically updated whenever SCHADS Award rates change.
How does the DEX / DSS reporting automation work?+
SupportIQ automatically collects DEX (Data Exchange) data directly from your service delivery records and submits reports to DSS on schedule. This completely eliminates manual data entry, spreadsheet exports, and missed DSS reporting deadlines.
Can SupportIQ replace CareMaster or other NDIS software?+
Yes. SupportIQ is a complete replacement for CareMaster and similar platforms, covering participant management, billing, rostering, caregiver mobile app, DEX reporting, and payroll in one AI-powered platform. Full migration support is included — we handle the data transfer for you.
How long does it take to go live with SupportIQ?+
Most providers go live within 4 weeks. This includes migrating data from your existing system, configuring the 30+ AI agents for your organisation, staff training, and full go-live support from our team.
What happens when NDIS price guide rates change?+
SupportIQ automatically updates all billing rules when the NDIA publishes new price guide rates. Your claims always use the current correct rates — no manual updates, no spreadsheet changes, no risk of billing at old rates.
Is my participant and billing data secure on SupportIQ?+
Yes. SupportIQ runs on Google Cloud Platform in the australia-southeast1 region — all data stays in Australia. Every agent action produces a full audit trail compliant with NDIS Commission requirements, including rule traces and version-locked records.
