# SupportIQ Pricing

## Plans and Pricing

SupportIQ offers three plans designed to match the scale of your NDIS service delivery operation.

---

### Starter — $399 per month

**For:** NDIS providers with up to 20 funded participants

**Includes everything in the platform:**
- NDIS billing automation and PRODA submission
- SCHADS Award payroll engine
- DEX compliance and XML generation
- Smart rostering with SCHADS rule enforcement
- Caregiver mobile app (iOS and Android)
- CEO analytics dashboard
- Participant and family portal
- Audit readiness vault

**Support:** Email and chat support

**No setup fee. Month-to-month — no lock-in contract.**

---

### Growth — $799 per month

**For:** NDIS providers with up to 40 funded participants

**Includes everything in Starter, plus:**
- Priority support response
- Onboarding assistance
- Advanced reporting and export options

**Support:** Email, chat, and phone support

**No setup fee. Month-to-month — no lock-in contract.**

---

### Enterprise — Custom pricing

**For:** Larger NDIS providers with more than 40 participants, multi-site organisations, and providers requiring custom configurations

**Includes everything in Growth, plus:**
- Unlimited participants
- White-label options (platform branded for your organisation)
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Custom integrations with your existing systems
- Data migration from CareMaster or other legacy systems
- Custom reporting and API access
- SLA-backed uptime guarantee

**Support:** Dedicated success manager with direct contact

---

## What Every Plan Includes

There are no feature tiers in SupportIQ. Every plan — including Starter — includes the full platform. The plans differ only by participant count, not by which modules you can access.

This means a provider on the Starter plan has access to the same AI billing agents, SCHADS engine, DEX module, mobile app, and analytics as an Enterprise client.

## No Hidden Costs

- No per-worker fees
- No per-claim fees
- No setup fee on Starter and Growth
- GCP infrastructure costs are included in the plan price
- Rate updates (NDIS price guide, SCHADS Award) are included — no annual update fees

## Migration Support

Providers moving from CareMaster, Lumary, ShiftCare, or other legacy systems can have their participant and historical data migrated. Migration support is included in Enterprise and available as an add-on for Growth.

## Billing

Plans are billed monthly. There is no annual prepayment required on Starter and Growth, though annual billing options with a discount are available on request for Growth and Enterprise.
