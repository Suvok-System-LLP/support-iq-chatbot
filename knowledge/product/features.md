# SupportIQ Platform Features

## Overview

SupportIQ is a purpose-built AI platform for Australian NDIS service providers. It replaces legacy tools like CareMaster with a fully integrated system that automates the compliance and operational work that currently consumes hours of admin time every week.

Every module is connected — data captured in one part of the system flows automatically into others. A shift logged on the caregiver app feeds into billing, payroll, DEX reporting, and the CEO dashboard simultaneously.

---

## NDIS Billing Automation

SupportIQ's billing engine creates and submits NDIS payment requests automatically from shift records.

**What it does:**
- Creates payment requests from completed and approved shifts
- Validates every claim against the current NDIS Support Catalogue and price guide before submission
- Catches errors before they reach PRODA — wrong codes, expired plans, rate mismatches
- Submits directly to the NDIA via the NDIS API (no manual PRODA data entry)
- Tracks payment status in real time
- Handles bulk submission for multiple participants in a single run
- Manages short notice cancellation claims automatically

**The result:** Billing cycle reduced from days to hours. Rejection rate near zero. No manual data entry in PRODA.

---

## SCHADS Payroll Engine

A complete Award interpretation and payroll calculation engine built specifically for SCHADS.

**What it does:**
- Stores each worker's classification level, employment type, and qualifications
- Calculates ordinary, penalty, and overtime rates for every shift automatically
- Applies casual loading where applicable
- Calculates all applicable allowances (travel, broken shift, first aid, on-call, sleepover)
- Produces itemised payslips showing every component
- Exports payroll data directly to Xero and MYOB
- Updates automatically when Fair Work Commission adjusts rates annually

**The result:** Zero underpayment risk. Zero overpayment. Audit-ready payslips for every worker for every pay period.

## DEX Compliance Module

End-to-end DEX reporting automation from session capture through to DSS XML submission.

**What it does:**
- Captures session data automatically from shift records
- Calculates SLKs from client records — no manual SLK calculation
- Prompts workers to collect SCORE data via the mobile app at period start and end
- Tracks SCORE completeness in real time — alerts when approaching the 95% threshold
- Generates DSS-compliant XML file at the end of each reporting period
- Validates the XML against the DEX schema before submission
- Maintains audit trail of all DEX submissions

**The result:** No manual DEX data entry. No spreadsheets. No missed deadlines. Completeness consistently above 95%.

---

## Smart Rostering

An intelligent rostering tool that builds SCHADS-compliant rosters with AI optimisation.

**What it does:**
- Drag-and-drop roster builder with SCHADS rules enforced in real time
- Alerts when a roster change would trigger overtime, break rules, or minimum engagement violations
- Auto-Fill button: AI optimises the roster to minimise penalty rate exposure while meeting participant requirements
- Worker availability and leave managed in the same system
- Credential expiry alerts — flags when a worker's NDIS Screening, First Aid, or other credential is expiring
- Sends shift notifications to workers via the mobile app

**The result:** Fewer rostering errors. Lower Sunday and public holiday labour costs. No manual compliance checking.

---

## Caregiver Mobile App

A mobile app designed for support workers, built for real-world field conditions.

**What it does:**
- GPS-verified clock-in and clock-out for every shift
- Shift details visible before arriving at a participant's location
- Guided SCORE data collection at period start and end — three questions, under 60 seconds
- Voice-to-text incident reporting with photo upload
- Case note entry from the field
- Offline mode: shift viewing and note drafting work without mobile data, sync when reconnected
- Available on iOS and Android

**The result:** Accurate shift records with GPS verification. SCORE data collected at the point of service. Incidents documented immediately rather than from memory hours later.

---

## CEO Analytics Dashboard

Real-time operational and financial intelligence for NDIS provider leadership.

**What it does:**
- Live NDIS revenue — billed, submitted, paid, outstanding
- Plan utilisation by participant — who is under-utilising, who is at risk of exhausting their plan early
- SCHADS labour cost breakdown by shift type, day, and worker
- DEX compliance status — current completeness percentage by outlet and program
- Incident and risk alerts generated by AI agents
- Staff credential compliance overview
- Financial KPIs: profit margin per support type, revenue trend, billing cycle time

**The result:** Leaders see the full operational picture in one place. No chasing reports from five different systems.
