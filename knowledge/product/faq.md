# SupportIQ FAQ

## General Questions

**Does SupportIQ replace CareMaster?**

Yes. SupportIQ is a full replacement for CareMaster and similar legacy systems. It covers participant management, rostering, shift management, NDIS billing, SCHADS payroll, DEX reporting, incident management, and reporting. Enterprise clients receive full data migration support to bring across historical participant records, staff profiles, and service history from CareMaster.

---

**How long does setup take?**

For Starter and Growth plans, most providers are fully operational within the same week. The onboarding process involves setting up your organisation profile, importing participant and staff records, and connecting your PRODA credentials. For Enterprise clients with large data sets and custom configurations, the typical timeline is 2 to 4 weeks including data migration.

---

**Is SupportIQ compliant with NDIS Practice Standards?**

Yes. SupportIQ is built to the NDIS Practice Standards and Quality Indicators. The audit readiness module maintains all records in the format required for NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission audits. The platform supports compliance with the Core Module, the High Intensity Daily Personal Activities module, and Support Coordination standards.

---

**Does SupportIQ integrate with Xero or MYOB?**

Yes. Payroll data calculated by the SCHADS engine exports directly to Xero and MYOB. The export includes all pay components — ordinary hours, penalty rates, allowances, and superannuation — broken down by worker and pay period. You do not need to re-enter payroll data in your accounting system.

---

**What happens when the NDIS price guide rates change?**

SupportIQ's price guide engine is updated whenever the NDIA publishes new rates. This happens automatically — you do not need to manually update any rates in the system. When rates change, the system applies the new rates to all new claims from the effective date and flags any in-progress claims that may be affected.

---

**Is our client data secure?**

Yes. All data is stored in Google Cloud Platform's `australia-southeast1` region (Sydney). Data never leaves Australian jurisdiction. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. The platform operates under ISO 27001-aligned security controls. SupportIQ complies with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the NDIS Practice Standards for privacy and dignity.

---

**Can SupportIQ handle multiple service types?**

Yes. SupportIQ supports all NDIS support categories — Assistance with Daily Life, Community Participation, Support Coordination, Therapeutic Supports, and others. It also handles Home Care and CHSP services for providers that deliver both aged care and NDIS programs.

---

**What about the NDIA's PACE system migration?**

SupportIQ is PACE-ready. The NDIA is progressively migrating participants from the old myplace system to the new PACE system. SupportIQ's billing engine handles both systems. Providers do not need to take any action when their participants are migrated to PACE — the system handles the differences automatically.

---

**Do support workers need technical training to use the mobile app?**

No significant training is required. The caregiver app is designed for workers who are not tech-savvy. The main functions — clock in, clock out, view shift, record a note, collect SCORE — are accessible from the home screen in three taps or fewer. Most workers are fully comfortable with the app after a single 10-minute walkthrough. The app is available in English and can be configured to support workers with accessibility needs.

**Can we import our existing participant data?**

Yes. SupportIQ supports CSV import for participant records, staff profiles, and service history. For providers migrating from CareMaster, a structured migration process is available through the Enterprise plan that brings across all historical data in a validated format. Growth plan clients can access migration support as an add-on service.

**What is the cancellation policy?**

Starter and Growth plans are month-to-month. You can cancel at the end of any billing month with no penalty. Your data is available for export for 30 days after cancellation. Enterprise contracts are governed by the specific terms in your service agreement.
